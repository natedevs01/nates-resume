Project Description

The Crown Prototype represents the final, refined version of my human-computer interaction project. At this stage, I iterated on the complete prototype, applying user feedback and evaluation results to improve usability, interaction flow, and design quality. The purpose of the Crown Prototype is to deliver a polished, near-final product that demonstrates my ability to apply HCI principles through iterative design and testing.
